Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where will the ceremony and reception be held?

Our wedding will be held at Calaveras Big Trees State Park in Arnold, CA. Our Ceremony will be located at the Standing Centennial Tree. The reception will be followed in the Jack Knight Hall.

2. Can I bring a plus one?

We are not allowing additional plus-ones to our wedding, as there is a max capacity at our reception hall. The bride or groom will have let you know if you are able to bring a plus one.

3. Should I bring a gift?

As we prepare to embark on our new journey together, your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for. However, if you would like to contribute to our special day, we have created a honeymoon fund instead of registering for traditional gifts, which can be found on our wedding website. Your donation will help make our honeymoon unforgettable and create memories that will last a lifetime. However, if you would like to bring a physical gift we have provided a small registry. We have provided a link to our honeymoon fund on our website!

4. What is the attire for the wedding?

We ask that our guests dress formal casual for our wedding, we want each person to be comfortable and confident in what they are wearing. If you would like some inspiration for what to wear you can follow the link to our guest mood board. Dress as extravagant and colorful as you would like!

5. What time will the ceremony be starting and the reception end?

The ceremony will be starting at 2pm, the reception will conclude at 9pm as the venue requires events to conclude by 10pm

6. Will there be other events to attend on the wedding weekend?

All our guests are invited to join us for a welcome lake party the day before on the 26th, this will be a casual lake party held at the Arnold Moose Lodge. We will have light food, a fire for s’mores, activities, as well kayaks, paddle boards, and floaties for the lake.

7. When is the RSVP deadline?

You can RSVP on our wedding website! The deadline to RSVP will be April, 14th 2026.

8. Are there accommodations nearby?

Yes! There are a variety of accommodations within Arnold. There are options for cabin rentals, hotel or B&B stays, as well as camping! You can follow this link to view options for accommodations!
